About Pack 3547

We're all about encouraging boys and girls to make friends, be helpful to others, and do their very best no matter the outcome. With the help of powerful learning projects and exciting outdoor activities, we aim to teach children that doing their best can be a fun and rewarding experience — no matter the difficulty of the challenge.

The Cub Scout program lets families have fun together while getting to know other families in your school and neighborhood. Adventure is calling: go camping, take a field trip somewhere new, and learn valuable life skills!

No matter what grade your child is in, Scouting is a blast. Cub Scouts is an affordable activity for all — no youth is turned away due to a family's inability to pay.

Ranks in Our Pack

Each rank is one school year of adventures, building on the last.

Lion

Kindergarten

A fun introduction to Scouting for kindergartners, done alongside an adult partner. Builds a foundation of character through monthly adventures.

Tiger

1st grade

An exciting program for energetic first graders. Tigers do stuff — lots of it — all with their adult partners, opening up the world to curious minds.

Wolf

2nd grade

Second graders explore hiking, playing games, communication skills, and our duty to country as they work toward the Wolf rank.

Bear

3rd grade

All about adventure! Bears complete themed, multidisciplinary "adventures" plus safety exercises to earn their rank badge.

Webelos

4th grade

We'll Be Loyal Scouts. Fourth graders learn outdoor skills and take on many adventures, earning the Webelos handbook, neckerchief, and slide.

Arrow of Light

5th grade

For the oldest Cub Scouts — 5th graders earn a patch that transfers to their Scouts BSA uniform, showing readiness for the next step on the trail.
